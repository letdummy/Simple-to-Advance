About Us

The world has awakened to this decade with a new disease outbreak, the novel coronavirus which has hit the world like a truck, people have lost their jobs, lost their loved ones, and the ones who have recovered from the virus, haven’t recovered properly.

We, The Beginners aimed to start “live” because we wanted people to have a platform where they can come and practice ways to be less stressed, and live better.

Our team comprises over 600 psychologists and psychiatrists that are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for any type of assistance or acute emergency. Even if all you need is someone to chat to, they are available.

We hope to keep track of those who are on the edge of suicide with our daily record so that we may intervene and help them find the will to live.

All data entered by users who are under code red and code orange will be accessible to our psychologists and psychiatrists. So that they are aware of the user's personal profiles and can prohibit them from ending their lives.

We believe that by introducing "live," we will be able to save as many lives as possible and encourage our users to exercise self-love and other healthy behaviours that will improve their lives.
