Our Story

It all started in 1990s

Tasty plus is a well-established and reputed restaurant located in the heart of Pokhara city,Bijaypur. It has dining hall with several private seating areas with total capacity of 50 people. We offer you varieties of Nepali, Newari, Indian and continental cuisine with traditional music and classical dancing. The restaurant ensures the perfect dining experience with an array of cuisine option. The restaurant is open daily all year around from 02:00pm till 10:00pm. The restaurant regularly holds mega functions like seminar, meeting, wedding, initiation ceremony, beauty contest etc. The restaurant provides a wide range of menu based on demand that could include "a la carte" or fixed menu with Nepali, Newari, Indian, chinese & continental cuisine.

Fresh and Delicious Nepali Dishes to delight your Friends and Family.
